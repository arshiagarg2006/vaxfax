About the Site

Our focus is on spreading correct vaccination information and keeping users up to date with the very thing that saves lives. When users enter their state, vaccine, and phone number, Vaxfax will take the stress off of parents and keep the focus on their child's safety. A scheduled test will be sent on August 1st (right before school), resources will be available to provide an overview of the vaccines, and vaccine coverage by state will be displayed. Stay safe, with Vaxfax!.
